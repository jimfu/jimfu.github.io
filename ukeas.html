I redesigned the whole website.

In UKEAS, we have couples of websites in different nations, except for official site, we not only have two grand fairs - Study World and Unitour, and also plenty events. In this time, I planned with our head of marketing and counselors, from wireframe to interface crafting, I did the prototype by inVision and re-organized the mass content.

Feed back matters.

Before the site launched, I drafted a form by Typeform and arrange several meetings with students to get treasure feedback from these leads. It’s very useful to know things what students really care and which could attract them strongly. After that, I also made a brief of the results and pivoted the idea we had discussed.
When the new website goes live, the marketing keeps eyes on Google Analytic and Facebook Page, to get more precise users behavior feed back, it could help us to improve the user experience.
Feed back always matters.
