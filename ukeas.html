Complete website re-design.

UKEAS has a number of websites, each related to the country in which it has offices. In addition to general official websites, the company runs large-scale education exhibitions (Study World and Unitour) which also have their own websites. During my time at the company I planned out website revamps with the Head of Marketing and Counselors, which involved wireframe design and crafting new interfaces. I made the prototype using inVision and successfully re-organized all of the sites' (considerable!) content.

Feedback matters!

Before launching the new site, I drafted a detailed questionnaire using Typeform, and arranged several meetings with student focus groups to garner feedback from them. Understanding what they wanted was really useful, and it ultimately helped make the site more attractive to them. After the focus group sessions I gave briefings on the results and highlighted the ideas that had been discussed.
Once the new website went live my colleagues in marketing kept a close eye on Google Analytics and traffic from social media to get detailed user behaviour feedback, which helped feed into a constant improvement loop.
Feedback always matters.

Modular template
